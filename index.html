About DEVSOC 21

DEVSOC is CodeChef-VIT’s annual flagship event hoping to empower young minds by bringing enthusiastic technocrats and thinkers under one roof. This year, the second iteration of the hackathon is being held with a goal to create a sprint like event, where participants create, hack, innovate to solve problems while adhering to the spirit of creativity and teamwork. The event is spread over three days, with the hackathon lasting 48 hours consisting of amazing swags, prizes, food and drinks!

FAQ

What's the cost of the hackathon?

DEVSOC 21 is completely free thanks to our sponsors! We'll also provide you with enough swags, drinks, meals throughout the hackathon.

What's the application process like?

You're required to register with us on our Hacker portal where you'll be asked for basic details and your resume. We’ll get to know about your abilities from your past projects, GitHub profile, participation/awards in other hackathons. We'll shortlist teams for the final hackathon round through this.

I'm a fresher and I don't have a good resume. Will I be selected?

We will be evaluating everyone based on different metrics. We'll take into account if you're a fresher and where you stand amongst your peers. If you don't have anything technical to add to your resume, feel free to add any public speaking events or other competitions you've been to.

Can I participate alone?

No! This hackathon is strictly a team competition where you can have 2-5 members in your team. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills

Do I need to have specific qualifications to be a part of the hack?

If you love to code, you're more than welcome to participate in the hackathon.

Can we use an existing projects or use the projects in other hackathons?

You are required to code the entire project from scratch, only after the hackathon begins. You are also strictly required to submit the project in only one hackathon.
